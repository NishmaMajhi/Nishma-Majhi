What is BIM

The bachelor of Information Management(BIM) is four-year eight semester program of faculty of Management , Tribhuvan University offering integrated Information Technology , and management courses.This courses is envisioned to enable student to develop skills in information technology , and then apply the theories and concepts of a broad, integrated curriculum covering computer, bussiness fundamentals and critical and analytical decision making .
